Registration Procedure

Step 1

A POC from each center submits the Registration Google Form, Mayapur Summer Camp Team receives POC details. If your center is not found in the Form please reach out to us and we will add.

Step 2

After form submit POC will receive a link to the form response, which can be edited later. POC also receives a mail from Mayapur Summer Camp Team, with Center ID and a google Spreadsheet Link to fill the detail of the participants from their center. POC can keep filling these details. 10th of April, this spreadsheet will no longer be accessible.

Step 3

Mayapur Summer Camp Team will process the details of the participants from each center and will cross verify the registration amount calculated on the shared sheet

Step 4

POC to pay the registration amount, payment process is launched on 16 March

Step 5

Book Accommodation for your center participants and pay the respective amount, booking will only be considered when amount is paid in full
